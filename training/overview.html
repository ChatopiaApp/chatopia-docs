Training System Overview

The training system allows you to improve your character NFTs by enhancing their capabilities and limits. Training uses CHATOPIA tokens to randomly select and upgrade one of your character's eight statistics.

How Training Works​

Random Selection Process​

Training operates through a random improvement system:

  • Random Stat Selection: System randomly chooses one of eight character stats to improve
  • No Control: Cannot choose which specific stat gets upgraded
  • Repeatable Process: Multiple training sessions needed for comprehensive development

Training Requirements​

Each training session requires specific resources:

  • Character Ownership: Must own the NFT you want to train
  • CHATOPIA Tokens: Required payment for each training operation
  • Connected Wallet: Web3 wallet linked to platform for transaction processing

Training Limitations​

Platform includes several training constraints:

  • Token Costs: CHATOPIA tokens consumed per training session
  • Random Outcomes: Cannot predict or control which stat improves
  • Training Timer: Waiting period after initiating training before receiving improvements

What Training Improves​

Content Limit Stats​

Training can enhance the maximum text allowed for character elements:

Name Length: 15 → 30 characters maximum
Tagline Length: 30 → 50 characters maximum
Description Length: 200 → 500 characters maximum
Greeting Length: 500 → 2,000 characters maximum
Definition Length: 500 → 30,000 characters maximum

Performance Stats​

Training can improve functional capabilities that affect character operation:

Memory Depth: 10 → 50 past messages retained
Concurrent Users: 100 → 1,000,000 simultaneous users supported
Daily Messages: 50 → 200 free messages per user per day

Training Process​

Step 1: Character Selection​

Access your character collection:

  • Navigate to character portfolio
  • Select character you want to train
  • Verify character ownership and current stats
  • Confirm sufficient CHATOPIA tokens for training

Step 2: Training Execution​

Complete training operation:

  • Choose "Train Character" option from character interface
  • Review training cost and token requirements
  • Confirm transaction through connected wallet
  • Training timer begins after successful transaction

Step 3: Results Review​

After training timer completion:

  • View which stat was randomly improved
  • See updated character capabilities and limits
  • Monitor cumulative training progress across sessions
  • Plan future training based on character development goals

Training Economics​

Cost Structure​

Training operations involve several cost considerations:

  • Token Requirements: CHATOPIA tokens required per training session
  • Transaction Fees: Blockchain gas costs for smart contract interactions
  • Cumulative Investment: Multiple sessions needed for comprehensive character development

Value Creation​

Training creates permanent improvements to character capabilities:

  • Enhanced Limits: Improved stats enable richer character development
  • Performance Gains: Better capabilities can increase user engagement
  • Asset Enhancement: Trained characters have improved capabilities and marketplace positioning

Platform Interface​

Accessing Training​

Training functionality available through character management interface:

  • Character collection displays current stats
  • Training options accessible from individual character pages
  • Progress tracking shows cumulative improvements over time
  • Transaction history records all training sessions

Training Results​

After each training session:

  • Platform displays which stat was improved
  • Updated character limits reflected immediately
  • Training history maintains record of all improvements
  • Character interface shows cumulative development progress
