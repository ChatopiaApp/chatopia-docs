Character Trading Guide

Learn how to buy and sell character NFTs on the Chatopia marketplace. This guide covers character evaluation, transaction processes, and platform safety features.

Understanding Character Value​

Character Statistics Overview​

Every character NFT has eight statistics that determine its capabilities and market value:

Content Statistics:

  • Name Length: Character limit for character names (15-30 characters)
  • Tagline Length: Limit for memorable catchphrases (30-50 characters)
  • Description Length: Space for background details (200-500 characters)
  • Greeting Length: Welcome message capacity (500-2,000 characters)
  • Definition Length: Personality framework space (500-30,000 characters)

Performance Statistics:

  • Memory Depth: Past messages retained in conversations (10-50 messages)
  • Concurrent Users: Simultaneous chat capacity (100-1,000,000 users)
  • Daily Messages: Free messages per user daily (50-200 messages)

What Affects Character Pricing​

Training Investment:

  • All characters start with identical base stats
  • Training sessions permanently improve character capabilities
  • Higher stats typically command higher marketplace prices

Performance Metrics:

  • Concurrent Users: Directly impacts revenue generation potential
  • Memory Depth: Affects conversation quality and user satisfaction
  • Daily Messages: Influences user engagement and accessibility

Character Quality:

  • Personality design and user appeal
  • Proven user engagement and interaction history
  • Market demand for specific character types

How to Buy Characters​

Before You Buy​

Research the Character:

  1. Review all eight statistics and any training history
  2. Check character personality and design quality
  3. Compare pricing with similar characters
  4. Verify seller reputation and transaction history

Prepare Your Wallet:

  • Ensure sufficient tokens for purchase price and transaction fees
  • Connect your Web3 wallet to the platform
  • Understand total costs including marketplace fees

Purchase Process​

Step 1: Browse and Filter

  • Use marketplace filters to find characters matching your criteria
  • Sort by statistics, price range, or character type
  • Review character details and seller information

Step 2: Evaluate and Decide

  • Examine character stats and training investment
  • Consider the character's role in your collection
  • Verify authenticity and ownership history

Step 3: Complete Purchase

  • Review transaction details and total costs
  • Confirm purchase through your connected wallet
  • Wait for blockchain confirmation and ownership transfer

After Purchase​

  • Verify character appears in your wallet and platform profile
  • Plan any training or development strategy
  • Monitor character performance and user engagement

How to Sell Characters​

Preparing Your Character for Sale​

Gather Information:

  • Document current statistics and any training history
  • Compile performance data if available
  • Research current market pricing for similar characters

Choose Listing Method:

  • Fixed Price: Set a specific sale price
  • Auction: Let buyers bid for optimal price discovery
  • Offers: Accept offers from interested buyers

Listing Process​

Step 1: Create Listing

  • Select the character you want to sell
  • Choose your preferred sale method and pricing
  • Add description highlighting character strengths

Step 2: Manage Your Listing

  • Respond to buyer inquiries promptly
  • Consider reasonable offers and negotiation
  • Monitor market conditions and adjust if needed

Step 3: Complete Sale

  • Accept buyer offer or auction result
  • Confirm transaction through platform escrow
  • Transfer character ownership upon payment confirmation

Post-Sale​

  • Verify payment received and character transferred
  • Update your portfolio records
  • Consider feedback and reputation building

Marketplace Features​

Search and Discovery​

Filter Options:

  • Sort by character statistics and capabilities
  • Filter by price range and character type
  • Search by specific traits or keywords

Character Information:

  • Complete statistical breakdown and training history
  • Performance metrics and user engagement data
  • Transparent pricing and transaction history

Transaction Security​

Platform Protection:

  • Secure escrow system for all transactions
  • Smart contract protection for buyers and sellers
  • Transparent transaction history on blockchain

Verification Systems:

  • Character authenticity confirmation
  • Seller reputation tracking
  • Community reporting for suspicious activity

Safety and Best Practices​

Due Diligence Checklist​

Before Buying:

  • ✅ Verify character statistics match listing description
  • ✅ Research seller transaction history and reputation
  • ✅ Understand all costs including fees and future development
  • ✅ Compare pricing with similar characters

Before Selling:

  • ✅ Research current market pricing for your character type
  • ✅ Ensure accurate character description and statistics
  • ✅ Understand marketplace fees and transaction costs
  • ✅ Set realistic pricing expectations

Transaction Safety​

Security Measures:

  • Always use platform's escrow system for transactions
  • Verify all transaction details before confirming
  • Keep records of all purchases and sales for tracking
  • Report suspicious activity to platform support

Common Precautions:

  • Start with smaller transactions while learning the marketplace
  • Never share your wallet private keys or seed phrases
  • Double-check wallet addresses and transaction amounts
  • Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true

Marketplace Fees and Costs​

Transaction Costs​

Platform Fees:

  • Reduced fees compared to external NFT marketplaces
  • Transparent fee structure with no hidden costs
  • Standard blockchain gas fees for all transactions

Additional Considerations:

  • Factor in potential future training costs for character development
  • Consider breeding opportunities and remaining breeding slots
  • Account for market volatility in pricing decisions
